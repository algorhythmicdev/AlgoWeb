Who we are

AlgoRhythmics is a Latvia-based, pre-company stage initiative building accessible AI tools, consulting pilots, and education programmes. We stay transparent about our build status and choose partners who value measurable progress.

  • Mission: unlock useful AI for everyday people, schools, startups, and small businesses across Latvia and beyond.
  • Operating from Daugavpils and Riga with a remote-first mindset and a 50/50 co-leadership model.
  • Rooted in Reclame Fabriek’s Signage Research Department for real-world R&D discipline.

Three pillars, one rhythm

Products, consulting, and education move together so partners see impact and skills growth quickly.

  • NodeVoyage travel OS and Ideonautix founder toolkit are in active build.
  • Consulting sprints map automation, integrate AI safely, and train teams to own the outputs.
  • Education programmes with LIAA align classroom pilots, teacher training, and community events.

Why this matters

Accessible AI only works when it stays transparent, inclusive, and measurable. That is our baseline.

  • Useful AI tools that explain themselves, respect autonomy, and support multiple languages.
  • Structured programmes with measurable outcomes for schools and cultural partners.
  • Focused startup support that removes noise and protects time.
  • Fair, local-friendly pricing that scales outward with proof.

How we build

Our stack combines signage-grade reliability with modern product cadence. Every layer tracks accessibility, privacy, and documentation.

Frontend

  • SvelteKit + TypeScript with WCAG 2.2 AA/AAA coverage, responsive layouts, and light/dark modes.
  • Multilingual interface (EN/LV/RU/UK/FR/ES) using shared tokens and motion guidelines tuned for accessibility.

Backend & Cloud

  • Google Cloud stack (Cloud Run, Cloud SQL/Postgres, Firestore, Storage, Redis) with Fastify APIs and Prisma.
  • OpenAPI-first contracts, CI/CD pipelines, and observability baked in.

AI Layer

  • Vertex AI as primary, OpenAI as fallback, with on-device models when privacy matters.
  • Explainable prompts, opt-out controls, and clear audit trails for every decision.

Realtime & Quality

  • WebSockets and SSE for collaboration, plus testing from unit to accessibility checks.
  • CI pipelines mirror signage QA discipline with scenario scripts and visual diffing.

Security & privacy

  • GDPR-first, encryption everywhere, role-based access, and monitoring for every release.
Vision & goals

Where we are heading next

Our roadmap stays public so partners can see how products, education pilots, and consulting add up to one accessible AI platform.

Product milestones

NodeVoyage and Ideonautix release in stages: alpha with guided pilots, beta with multilingual rollouts, and general availability once the shared access platform is proven.

  • Alpha builds focus on collaborative planning, explainable AI, and mobile capture.
  • Public beta targets June 2026 with Latvian, English, and Russian support.
  • Pricing stays under €3/month with family and student discounts from day one.

Education targets

Together with LIAA partners we grow AI literacy across Latvia before expanding to the wider region.

  • Reach 80% of institutions with adaptable lesson packs and teacher training.
  • Support 25k+ students per year with project-based learning and bilingual resources.
  • Train 500+ educators with mentorship cohorts and follow-up assessments.

Consulting outcomes

Consulting stays tightly scoped: we co-design, build, and hand over so in-house teams stay confident long after the engagement.

  • Each pilot starts with a shared readiness scorecard and success metrics.
  • Delivery includes documentation, governance rituals, and opt-out controls.
  • We remain on-call for three months to mentor teams as they take full ownership.
